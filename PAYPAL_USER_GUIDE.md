## How to Pay with PayPal

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Add Products to Cart
1. Browse products on BuyJan
2. Click "Add to Cart" on your desired items
3. Review items in your cart

#### Step 2: Go to Checkout
1. Click "Checkout" button
2. Verify your cart items and quantities
3. Proceed to shipping information

#### Step 3: Enter Shipping Details
1. Enter your full name
2. Enter your shipping address
3. Enter your phone number (for delivery coordination)
4. Click "Continue"

#### Step 4: Select PayPal Payment
1. On the payment method screen, select **"PayPal"**
2. Review your order summary:
   - Subtotal
   - Tax amount
   - Shipping cost
   - **Total amount (in OMR)**

#### Step 5: Click PayPal Button
1. Click the **"Pay with PayPal"** button
2. A PayPal login window will open

#### Step 6: Log In to PayPal
1. Enter your PayPal email address
2. Enter your PayPal password
3. Click "Log In"
4. **Or**: Click "Sign up" if you don't have a PayPal account yet

#### Step 7: Review Payment Details
1. PayPal shows your:
   - Order amount
   - Currency (OMR)
   - Shipping address (if available)
   - Item details
2. Review all information carefully
3. Click **"Approve"** or **"Continue"** button

#### Step 8: Confirm Payment
1. You'll be returned to BuyJan
2. Payment is being processed...
3. You'll see confirmation message

#### Step 9: Order Confirmation
1. You'll receive an order confirmation page
2. Your order number will be displayed
3. **Check your email** for order confirmation
4. **Save your order number** for reference

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Registration

**Q: Do I need a PayPal account to buy from BuyJan?**  
A: Yes, you need a PayPal account to use PayPal payment method. You can create a free PayPal account at www.paypal.com if you don't have one.

**Q: How do I create a PayPal account?**  
A: Visit www.paypal.com and click "Sign Up". You'll need:
- Valid email address
- Phone number
- Personal identification
- Linked bank account or card

**Q: Is PayPal available in Oman?**  
A: Yes, PayPal is available in Oman. You can create an account using your Omani phone number and bank account.

### Security & Privacy

**Q: Is it safe to use PayPal on BuyJan?**  
A: Yes, PayPal uses bank-level encryption to protect your information. Your payment details are never shared with BuyJan.

**Q: Will BuyJan see my payment information?**  
A: No. PayPal handles all payment processing. BuyJan only receives confirmation that payment succeeded, not your card/bank details.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes, BuyJan uses HTTPS encryption and complies with payment security standards (PCI-DSS).

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**  
A: PayPal will show you the reason (expired card, insufficient funds, etc.). No charges are made. Try again with different payment method.

### Payments & Refunds

**Q: What should I do if I don't see the payment confirmation?**  
A: Check your email (including spam folder) for order confirmation. If you don't receive it within 5 minutes:
1. Go back to BuyJan checkout
2. Check if order was created (you may see confirmation page)
3. Contact our support team with your PayPal transaction ID

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Yes, refunds are available according to BuyJan's refund policy. Contact our support team to request a refund.

**Q: How long does refund take?**  
A: Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days:
1. BuyJan processes refund
2. PayPal receives refund authorization
3. Money returned to your PayPal account
4. PayPal transfers to your bank (may take additional 1-2 business days)

**Q: What is PayPal Buyer Protection?**  
A: PayPal Buyer Protection protects you if:
- Item not received
- Item significantly not as described
- Unauthorized transaction

If you have issues, you can open a PayPal dispute case.

### Currency & Pricing

**Q: Why are prices in OMR?**  
A: BuyJan is based in Oman and prices are in Omani Rial (OMR), the local currency.

**Q: Will PayPal convert OMR to my currency?**  
A: If you're paying from a non-OMR PayPal account, PayPal can convert currency for you. You'll see the conversion rate at checkout.

**Q: Are there currency conversion fees?**  
A: PayPal may charge a conversion fee if converting currency. The exact rate and fee will be shown during payment approval.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The PayPal window didn't open. What should I do?**  
A: This may be a browser issue:
1. Check if pop-ups are blocked in your browser
2. Disable ad blockers temporarily
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Use incognito/private browsing mode

**Q: I'm stuck on the PayPal approval page. What should I do?**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Wait 30 seconds for page to load
2. Check your internet connection
3. Refresh the page (don't click back)
4. Close and reopen the page
5. Try again from the BuyJan checkout

**Q: What if my payment was approved but order wasn't created?**  
A: This is rare but can happen:
1. PayPal took your payment
2. BuyJan may not have received confirmation
3. Check your email for order confirmation
4. Check your BuyJan account for orders
5. Contact support with your PayPal transaction ID

### Orders & Delivery

**Q: When will I receive my order?**  
A: Order delivery time depends on:
- Order processing: 1-2 business days
- Shipping method: 2-7 business days in Oman
- You'll receive tracking information via email

**Q: Can I cancel or modify my order?**  
A: Order cancellation depends on processing status:
- **Not yet processed** (< 1 hour): Can cancel via account
- **Processing** (1-24 hours): Contact support immediately
- **Shipped**: Cannot cancel, but can refuse delivery

**Q: Can I change the delivery address?**  
A: Address change depends on order status:
- **Not yet processed**: Change in your account
- **Processing**: Contact support within 1 hour
- **Already shipped**: Cannot change

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Payment system is not available"

**Cause**: PayPal integration is not configured  
**Solution**:
- This is a temporary technical issue
- Try again in a few minutes
- Use different payment method if available
- Contact support

### Problem: "Failed to create payment order"

**Cause**: Invalid order information  
**Check**:
- All addresses are filled correctly
- No special characters in name field
- Email address is valid
- All required fields are filled
- Order total is positive number

**Solution**:
- Click "Back" to review order
- Correct any errors
- Try again

### Problem: "Payment capture failed"

**Cause**: PayPal payment was approved but capture failed  
**What happens**:
- PayPal took your payment
- BuyJan couldn't confirm receipt
- Money may be held temporarily by PayPal

**Solution**:
1. Check PayPal transaction history
2. Wait 24 hours (money returns automatically if held)
3. Contact BuyJan support with:
   - Transaction ID from PayPal
   - Time of transaction
   - Amount paid

### Problem: "Amount mismatch"

**Cause**: Order total changed between pages  
**Why this happens**:
- Cart changed (old browser session)
- Prices updated
- Promotional code expired

**Solution**:
- Clear browser cache
- Return to checkout
- Verify cart items haven't changed
- Proceed to payment again

### Problem: Duplicate Charges

**Cause**: Rare - payment processed twice  
**Prevent**:
- Don't click "Pay" button twice
- Don't use browser back button during payment
- Wait for confirmation page before closing

**If it happens**:
1. Check PayPal transaction history
2. One payment should be in "pending" status
3. Contact PayPal support to cancel duplicate
4. Contact BuyJan support

### Problem: Payment Timeout

**Cause**: Network or PayPal server issue  
**What to do**:
- Don't click anything yet
- Wait 2 minutes for processing
- Check your email for confirmation
- If timeout message appears:
  - Check your PayPal account (transaction may have gone through)
  - Contact support if unsure

**Prevention**:
- Use stable internet connection
- Avoid mobile network during checkout
- Don't close browser during payment

### Problem: PayPal Login Issues

**Cause**: Forgot password or account issues  
**Solution**:

**Forgot Password**:
1. Go to www.paypal.com
2. Click "Log In"
3. Click "Forgot password?"
4. Follow email instructions to reset
5. Return to BuyJan and try again

**Account Locked**:
1. Check your email from PayPal
2. Follow PayPal's unlock instructions
3. Contact PayPal support: www.paypal.com/support

**Wrong Account**:
1. Log out of PayPal (if paying from web)
2. Log back in with correct account
3. Try payment again
